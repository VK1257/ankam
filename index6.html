The 30+ Year Educational Journey

Early Teaching Roots

Began as a school teacher at **Saraswathi Vidya Mandir** (Kesava Nagar), fostering a love for learning in young minds.

Public Service in Schooling

Continued at **Punjagutta Government School** and served as a Maths Lecturer at **Nalanda Junior College**.

Foundational Leadership

Served as **Secretary of the Akhil Bharatiya Vidyarthi Parishad (ABVP)** at Andhra University, advocating for student needs.

Systemic Leadership: Shaping Policy and Influence

Educational Policy and Governance

As **State General Secretary of the APSFRCA**, he champions the interests of self-financed institutions, striving to create a more supportive and effective educational framework.

His commitment to leadership is poised to extend through his pursuit of a nomination as an **Executive Committee Member at Osmania University, Hyderabad**.

Journalism and Public Discourse

He has served as an **Educational Journalist** with prominent newspapers such as *Andhra Jyothi* and *Deccan Chronicle*.

He speaks on TV (*I News*, *ATV*) and writes for *EENADU* and *SAKSHI* on topics like the **Indian Education System** and **Career Guidance**.
